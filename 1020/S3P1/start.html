Tame.the.Cat()

How to Play

You found a mysterious black cat.
Use simple commands to gain its trust.
Cats are unpredictable — do your best to earn its trust.

  • feed(cat) – offer food
  • pet(cat) – try to pet the cat
  • play(cat) – play with a toy
  • wait() – give the cat some space
  • call(cat) – call out to the cat
  • debug(cat) – carefully observe its behavior

Each action rolls a 6-sided die, which determines how the cat reacts.
The outcome changes trust status, shown in the bar above the cat.
Reach full trust to tame the cat.
If trust drops to 0, the cat runs away.

Type commands in the box at the bottom of the screen and press Execute.
You can type help at any time to come back to this page.
